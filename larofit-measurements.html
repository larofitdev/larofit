📏 Measuring tips
Measure at the same time of day — morning before eating is best.
Use the same tape measure each time for consistency.
Measure every 4–6 weeks, not weekly — changes take time.
Waist is measured at the narrowest point, hips at the widest.
Arms and thighs should be measured flexed, at the largest point.
